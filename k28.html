In a Nutshell

I was part of the editorial team for the 28th issue of komma magazine. Komma is a platform for showcasing projects by students from the Faculty of Design at Mannheim University of Applied Sciences. The magazine is entirely organized by the students themselves, with all tasks managed exclusively by the editors. The composition of the team constantly changes, giving each issue its own theme and identity. Komma serves as a showroom for bachelor's, master's, semester, and independent projects. Each issue is distributed to leading figures in the design industry.
