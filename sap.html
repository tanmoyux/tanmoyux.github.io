In a Nutshell

As a UX Designer at SAP SE, my primary responsibility was managing a component library and designing new components as needed. SAP SE is a multinational software corporation and one of Germany’s most successful companies. I was part of the SIX Design & Piloting team, which focuses on creating high-impact showcases for the SAP Experience Centers and major events such as Sapphire, SAP’s flagship conference. My role ranged from maintaining and expanding the component library to illustrating keynote visuals and designing assets for various SAP departments.

But due to non-disclosure agreements, I am unable to share any of my work for SAP.
