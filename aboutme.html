Versatile designer with focus on UX

My name is Tanmoy Roy, a 25-year-old Master’s student in Communication Design at the University of Applied Sciences Mannheim. While my primary focus lies in UX design, I also have extensive experience in graphic design and illustration. Outside of my studies, I channel my creativity into drawing, writing, and selling comics, alongside taking on freelance projects. Feel free to check out my website for updates on my comic series.

What do I love the most about UX? The methodology behind it. Methods, guidelines, psychology and feedback will allow you to justify your design decisions and ensure the best optimized work for the user.

That’s why I describe my design process in my UX projects so much in depth.
