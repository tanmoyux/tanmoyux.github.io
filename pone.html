In a Nutshell

During my mandatory internship, I worked as a UX designer at the Mannheim online marketing company "PERFORMANCE ONE" for six months. I continued in this position as a working student for an additional semester. My tasks primarily involved design and analysis. Design-wise, I worked on website designs, implemented websites using WordPress, created general designs, and participated in the creation and redesign of the internal newsletter. Analytically, I contributed to UX analyses of existing sites, documenting the results in presentations. Additionally, I handled extra tasks such as sending internal newsletters. Here is a selection of projects:

SAMSUNG SDI EU Landingpage & Website

Client

SAMSUNG SDI EU

Year

03.2023 – 09.2023

What I did

Research, information architecture, wireframes, mockups, UX Design, UI Design, graphic design, atomic design system, feedback loops with clients, implementation with WordPress and Elementor

To the website

In a Nutshell

Development and implementation of a landing page and a microsite for SAMSUNG SDI EU, aimed at positioning them as an attractive employer. The pages follow Samsung's clean and sterile design style but are filled with interactivity. This was also the first website I created using WordPress and Elementor. In total, I designed nine pages each for desktop and mobile.

Year

08.2023

What I did

To the website

In a Nutshell

Design of an online portfolio for the grand opening of the fashion boutique "Story of a Lady" in Mannheim. P.ONE Newsletter Redesign

Client

PERFORMANCE ONE

Year

05.2023 – 10.2023

What I did

Research, mockups, UX design, UI design, graphic design, sendig of newsletter, process optimization, creation of templates

In a Nutshell

Redesign and process optimization of the "PERFORMANCE ONE" newsletter. In addition to a user-centric design, I was responsible for the monthly creation and distribution of the newsletter. Therefore, I revamped the outdated creation process, established a new workflow for increased efficiency, and created a template with instructions for future contributors. The design incorporates bold, interactive, and AI-generated elements to provide a modern and memorable user experience.

My year at PERFORMANCE ONE allowed for both personal and creative growth.
