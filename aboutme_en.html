My name is Tanmoy Roy, I am 24 years old, and I am currently pursuing my Master's in Communication Design at the University of Applied Sciences Mannheim. Here’s my other website if you want to keep track of my other works.

That’s why I describe my design process in my UX projects so much in depth.
